About Our Charity

We are so happy you are here and we introduce ourselves to you as the H2E Foundation "Habitat for humanity East", non profitable organisation in Uganda

Habitant for Humanity East; H2E was founded in 2024 after seeing a growing need from many vulnerable children and people that were connected to the habitant for Humanity East for help. Hisaalu heard his call and H2E was born. Over the past one year, Habitant for Humanity East has grown immensely. Thanks to the people who bring in generous donations which have helpe us support a number of families, children and women in Uganda particularly Eastern Region

Stationed in Mayuge District Uganda, our activities Community outreach, Health care, Sanitation, water, women empowerment programs an planning to put up a school to support the children in education, though still looking for more resources from both the local and international Community which will surely lead to a bridge between us and the needy fixed.

We aso aim at bringing Jesus/ God's gospel to the communities whih had lost hope in religion advantages most of them a predominately muslim but are really loving the amazing people of late.

We are blessed to have an amazing team in Mayuge/ on site made up of ten people who continously praise God and prioritise the needs of our Mayuge Community in everything they do.
